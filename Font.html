The Power of Dreams

In the still of the night, when the world is at rest,

Our minds come alive, doing what they do best.

They wander and whisper, they dance and they play,

They show us the hopes that we hide in the day.


So close your eyes gently, and give in to sleep,

Let your thoughts fly and let your soul leap.

For dreams are the magic, the stars in our mind,

That help us to heal, to grow and to find.

- By Ravi Shankar
